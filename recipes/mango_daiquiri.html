Mango Daiquiri

For this quick and easy mango daiquiri, use frozen mangoes which eliminates the need for ice and will not water down the drink. Switch it up and use any frozen fruit you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • fluid ounces rum
  • 2 fluid ounces freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 fluid ounces agave syrup

Steps to make your delicious Mango Daiquiri

  1. Place mangoes, rum, lime juice, and agave syrup in blender cup and blend until smooth.
